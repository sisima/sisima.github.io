about

ZI is a website that let you explore some little hisotry stories and evolution of the Chinese characters. The poem is from one of the Four Great Classical Novels of Chinese literature called Journey to the West. Basically，the poem is about how the world evolve and grow out of nothing by Pan Gu who is the creator of the universe in Chinese mythology.
